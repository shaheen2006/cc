CITY CENTER MANGALORE

introduction

Mangalore City Center is a bustling commercial area that offers a variety of shopping options, dining experiences, and entertainment facilities. It attracts visitors with its modern amenities and diverse offerings.

about us

The city center is designed to provide a comprehensive shopping and leisure experience, featuring a mix of local and international brands, food outlets, and recreational options. It aims to cater to the diverse preferences of shoppers and families looking for a day out.

Clothing brands

Levi's

Levi Strauss & Co., established in 1853, is renowned worldwide for its iconic denim jeans and casual apparel. Levi’s offers a diverse range of jeans, jackets, shirts, and accessories that blend durability with timeless style. Its products cater to men, women, and children, embodying a rugged yet fashionable aesthetic that has made it a staple in wardrobes globally.

Arrow

Arrow, founded in 1851, has carved a niche as a leading brand in men’s formal and casual wear. Known for its impeccable tailoring and sophisticated designs, Arrow offers a range of shirts, trousers, suits, and accessories that cater to professionals and discerning customers seeking quality and elegance in their attire.

Van Heusen

Van Heusen, a part of PVH Corp., is synonymous with modern business attire for men. Established with a focus on innovation and style, Van Heusen offers a variety of shirts, suits, ties, and accessories crafted from premium fabrics. Its collections reflect a blend of contemporary fashion trends and timeless elegance, making it a preferred choice among professionals and fashion-forward individuals.

FabIndia

FabIndia celebrates India’s rich cultural heritage by showcasing traditional crafts and textiles in contemporary designs. Founded with a commitment to sustainable practices and ethical sourcing, FabIndia offers a wide array of clothing for men, women, and children, along with home furnishings, personal care products, and accessories. Each product reflects the craftsmanship of rural artisans across India, promoting sustainable livelihoods and preserving traditional art forms.

Food Brands

McDonald's

McDonald's is the world's largest fast-food chain, recognized for its iconic burgers, fries, and beverages. Established with a focus on consistent quality and convenience, McDonald's menu includes a variety of fast-food options that appeal to all ages and tastes. It is known for its efficient service and global presence, making it a popular choice for quick meals and family dining.

KFC

Kentucky Fried Chicken (KFC), founded in 1952, is renowned for its secret blend of herbs and spices used in its signature fried chicken recipes. KFC offers a range of chicken pieces, sandwiches, sides like mashed potatoes and coleslaw, and desserts. Its menu caters to chicken enthusiasts seeking flavorful and satisfying dining experiences both in-store and through takeaway options.

Pizza Hut

Pizza Hut is a globally recognized pizza chain offering a variety of pizzas, pastas, and appetizers. Founded with a commitment to quality ingredients and customer satisfaction, Pizza Hut provides dine-in, takeaway, and delivery services. It is known for its diverse menu options and family-friendly dining atmosphere, making it a popular choice for casual meals and gatherings.

Subway

Subway is known for its made-to-order submarine sandwiches and fresh ingredients. Founded with a focus on healthier fast-food alternatives, Subway offers a customizable menu with a variety of breads, meats, cheeses, and toppings to suit individual preferences. Its emphasis on fresh and nutritious choices has made it a favorite among health-conscious consumers seeking quick and customizable meal options.
